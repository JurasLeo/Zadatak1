The Story of Central

Discover the fascinating history and highlights of Central, from its humble beginnings to its place as a global culinary icon.

Our Beginnings

Founded by the world-renowned chef Virgilio Martinez in Lima, Peru, Central Restaurant opened its doors in 2008. It was born from a passion for exploring Peru's unique biodiversity and transforming it into culinary art. Each dish tells a story of a different altitude, ecosystem, and culture within Peru.

Achievements

  • Ranked among the top 50 restaurants in the world multiple times.
  • Recipient of the prestigious Latin America’s Best Restaurant award.
  • Pioneered the "Mater Initiative" to source ingredients sustainably from local farmers and communities.

Did You Know?

Central serves dishes inspired by different altitudes in Peru, from the depths of the Pacific Ocean to the high Andes at 4,000 meters above sea level. Every plate is a culinary journey through Peru's rich ecosystems!
