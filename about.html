About Us

PTJP (Piti jesti piti) is more than just a platform for food; it's a culinary journey that celebrates the art and joy of eating. Offering inspiring recipes, helpful tips, expert guidance, perfect beverage pairings, and insightful insights into the world of food and drink, PTJP is designed to empower individuals to explore the diverse world of food with confidence and creativity. With a mission to turn every bite into a story and every meal into an opportunity to savor life's rich flavors, PTJP invites you to join a community of food enthusiasts on a journey of culinary exploration and discovery.

Our mission

Our mission at PTJP (Piti jesti piti) is to inspire and empower individuals to embrace the diverse world of food with confidence, creativity, and joy. We strive to provide a comprehensive platform that offers accessible resources, expert guidance, and a supportive community for home cooks, culinary enthusiasts, and food lovers alike. Through our curated collection of recipes, culinary tips, beverage pairings, insightful articles, and interactive experiences, we aim to foster a deeper appreciation for the art of cooking, the joy of eating, and the cultural significance of food. Our ultimate goal is to create a global community where everyone can share their passion for food, learn from one another, and embark on a delicious journey of culinary exploration and discovery.

Our story

In the heart of a bustling city, amidst the aromatic alleys of food markets and the sizzling sounds of kitchen experimentation, PTJP (Piti jesti piti) was conceived. It began with a simple idea—to create a digital sanctuary where food lovers could converge, share, and celebrate their passion for all things culinary. Fuelled by a collective desire to explore the diverse world of food, the founders of PTJP embarked on a journey of culinary discovery. They scoured the globe for the most tantalizing recipes, sought out expert advice from seasoned chefs, and delved deep into the cultural significance of cuisines from every corner of the earth.

Together, the PTJP community embarked on a delicious adventure, swapping recipes, exchanging cooking tips, and bonding over their shared culinary experiences. From cozy family dinners to extravagant feasts, every meal became an opportunity to connect, create, and savor the flavors of life.
